The Story

August 4, 2012

On an overcast morning in the Bay Area, Ryan picked up Lisa to go have lunch in Napa, CA. Lisa thought they were going to celebrate the two year anniversary of their first date, but unbeknownst to her, Ryan had other plans. Before they left for Napa, Ryan presented her with a gift; a necklace with a California-shaped pendant with a heart over Los Angeles.

In Napa, they had lunch at Ad Hoc Addendum, where they ate the famous fried chicken. After lunch, Ryan presented Lisa with another gift: tickets to the Hollywood Bowl to see Pixar in Concert and a trip to the Magic Castle! Lisa was startled, surprised, and confused that they were heading back to LA!

So they hopped on a plane and flew to Burbank. Lisa was excited to go to the Magic Castle. However, one needs a guest pass from a member to enter the castle, thus, Ryan told her they needed to go pick up the pass at a house of an acquaintance.

When they arrived at the house, Ryan urged Lisa to come inside with him. As she walked through the front door, she saw rose petals and knew something was awry. The rose petals made a path to a ladder on the balcony. On Ryan's request, they both went up to the roof, where Lisa saw a couch and table, laid out with a beautiful vase of hydrangea and candles, and a ring!

Then Ryan got down on one knee, with the view of Lisa's favorite skyline behind them, and asked Lisa to marry him. And she said yes.

After the proposal, they ate dinner on the balcony, overlooking the city of Los Angeles. They went to back to Tabitha's (Lisa's friend) house, where Lisa was again surprised with a celebration of their engagement with their friends and family.
